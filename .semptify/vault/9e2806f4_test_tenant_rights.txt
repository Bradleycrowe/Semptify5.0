# MINNESOTA TENANT RIGHTS OVERVIEW

## Introduction
This document provides essential information about tenant rights in Minnesota under Minn. Stat. § 504B.

## Notice Requirements
**14-Day Notice**: For nonpayment of rent, landlords must provide a 14-day written notice before filing an eviction action.

The notice must clearly state:
1. The amount of rent owed
2. The date by which payment must be made
3. That failure to pay will result in eviction proceedings

## Security Deposits
Under Minnesota law, landlords must:
- Return the deposit within 21 days after tenant moves out
- Provide itemized list of any deductions
- Hold deposits in a separate account

## Habitability Standards
Landlords are required to maintain:
- Working plumbing and heating
- Safe electrical systems
- Structural integrity
- Freedom from pests and rodents

## Retaliation Protection
Landlords cannot evict tenants in retaliation for:
- Reporting code violations
- Joining tenant organizations
- Exercising legal rights
