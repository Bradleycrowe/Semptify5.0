üìã Overview

Semptify is a free, open-source tenant rights assistance tool designed to help tenants understand their rights and navigate housing-related legal matters. We are committed to protecting your privacy by simply not collecting any personal information.

This privacy policy explains what data we do and don't collect, how the application works, and your rights as a user.

üíæ What's Stored Locally (On Your Device)

Semptify stores certain data locally on your device only using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage/IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

Data Type Storage Purpose
Anonymous Session ID Local Only A random ID to maintain your session (not linked to your identity)
Your Documents Local Only Documents you upload stay on your device unless you connect cloud storage
Timeline & Calendar Local Only Important dates and events you add
Notes & Checklists Local Only Personal notes you create
Preferences Local Only Your UI preferences and settings

‚òÅÔ∏è Optional Cloud Storage Integration

Semptify offers optional integration with third-party cloud storage providers:

  • Google Drive
  • Microsoft OneDrive
  • Dropbox

If you choose to connect cloud storage:

  • Your documents are stored in your own cloud account, not on our servers
  • We only store an encrypted access token to connect to your storage
  • You can disconnect at any time, and we immediately delete the access token
  • We never have direct access to your cloud storage contents

ü§ñ AI Assistant Features

  • AI responses are for informational purposes only and do not constitute legal advice
