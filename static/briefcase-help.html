Overview

The Document Briefcase is your command center for organizing and analyzing important tenant rights documents. It uses advanced AI-powered OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text from images, PDFs, and scanned documents, then helps you identify key information like dates, amounts, and addresses.

Import

Upload documents from your computer, Vault, or by dragging and dropping.

Analyze

AI extracts text and automatically identifies dates, amounts, addresses, and more.

Highlight

Color-code important information with different highlight types.

Timeline

Send extracted dates directly to the Timeline Builder for case tracking.

Getting Started

The Briefcase interface has three main panels:

Left Panel: Document Sources

  • Upload Zone - Drag & drop or click to upload new documents
  • Vault Documents - Access previously saved documents
  • Recent Files - Quick access to recently opened files

Center Panel: Document Viewer

  • View your document or extracted text
  • Zoom, rotate, and navigate pages
  • Select text to highlight important information

Right Panel: Extracted Fields

  • View automatically extracted information
  • Organize dates, parties, amounts, addresses, and notes
  • Edit labels and values as needed

Uploading Documents

Supported File Types

  • Images: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, WebP
  • Documents: PDF (scanned or text-based)

How to Upload

  1. Drag & Drop: Simply drag a file from your computer and drop it on the upload zone in the left panel.
  2. Click to Browse: Click the upload zone to open your file browser and select a document.
  3. From Vault: Click on any document in the "Vault Documents" section to load it into the viewer.

Tip

For best OCR results, use clear, high-resolution scans. Avoid blurry photos or documents with heavy shadows.

Smart Text Extraction (OCR)

Our multi-engine OCR system uses advanced AI to extract text from your documents with high accuracy, even from handwritten notes or poor-quality scans.

Processing Options

  • Quality Level:
    • Fast - Quick processing, good for clear documents
    • Balanced - Best for most documents (recommended)
    • Best - Maximum accuracy, slower processing
    • Legal - Optimized for legal documents with special terms
  • Document Type:
    • Auto-Detect - Let AI determine the best approach
    • Printed Text - Standard typed documents
    • Handwritten - Notes, signatures, forms filled by hand
    • Form/Table - Structured documents with fields
    • Legal Document - Leases, court papers, notices

How to Analyze

  1. Upload or select a document
  2. Choose your processing options (or leave on auto)
  3. Click the Analyze button
  4. Wait for processing - you'll see a progress bar
  5. Review extracted text and auto-detected fields

Auto-Extracted Information

The system automatically identifies and extracts:

  • Dates: Notice dates, deadlines, court dates
  • Amounts: Rent, fees, damages, deposits
  • Addresses: Property addresses, mailing addresses
  • Case Numbers: Court case references
  • Phone Numbers & Emails: Contact information

Highlighting & Annotations

Color-code important information in your documents for easy reference.

Highlight Colors

Date - Deadlines, court dates, notice dates

Party - Names of landlords, tenants, attorneys

Amount - Money amounts, rent, fees

Address - Property or mailing addresses

Legal - Legal terms, case numbers, statutes

Note - General annotations

How to Highlight

  1. Click the "Text Mode" toggle to enable text selection
  2. Select text in the document viewer by clicking and dragging
  3. A tooltip appears with highlight options
  4. Click the color/type you want to apply
  5. The highlighted text appears in the right panel under "Extracted Fields"

Pro Tip

Use the highlight mode buttons at the top to quickly highlight multiple items of the same type without re-selecting each time.

Timeline Integration

Send extracted dates directly to the Timeline Builder to track your case chronology.

How to Add Events to Timeline

  1. Analyze your document to extract dates
  2. Click the purple Timeline button in the save bar
  3. A modal appears showing all extracted dates
  4. Check the boxes next to dates you want to add
  5. Edit the event title if needed (click the text field)
  6. Select the event type from the dropdown:
    • Notice - Eviction notices, warnings
    • Payment - Rent due dates, payment records
    • Maintenance - Repair requests, inspections
    • Communication - Letters, emails, calls
    • Court - Hearings, filings, deadlines
    • Other - General events
  7. Click "Send to Timeline"

Adding Custom Events

If you notice a date that wasn't automatically extracted, you can add it manually:

  1. In the Timeline modal, find the "Add Custom Event" section
  2. Enter the date (e.g., "Dec 15, 2025" or "12/15/2025")
  3. Enter an event title
  4. Click "Add Custom Event"

Important

Events are saved to both the server (permanent) and your browser's local storage (for quick access in Timeline Builder). Open the Timeline Builder to see your added events!

Saving to Vault

Save your documents to the Semptify Vault for secure storage and easy access later.

How to Save

  1. After uploading and annotating your document
  2. Enter a document title in the field at the bottom
  3. Select a document category (Lease, Notice, Court, etc.)
  4. Click the Save to Vault button

Document Categories

  • Lease - Rental agreements, lease documents
  • Notice - Eviction notices, warnings, demands
  • Court - Court papers, summons, judgments
  • Communication - Letters, emails, texts
  • Financial - Rent receipts, invoices, bills
  • Maintenance - Repair requests, work orders
  • Evidence - Photos, documentation
  • Other - Miscellaneous documents

Pro Tips

Better Photos = Better Results

When photographing documents with your phone, ensure good lighting, hold the camera steady, and capture the entire document without cutting off edges.

Track Deadlines

Always send important dates to the Timeline Builder immediately. California eviction cases have strict deadlines - missing one can affect your rights!

Organize by Category

Use consistent document categories when saving to the Vault. This makes it easier to find documents later and generate reports for court.

Extract Everything

Even if a date or amount doesn't seem important now, highlight it anyway. Details that seem minor can become critical evidence in court.

Troubleshooting

OCR Not Working?

  • Image too blurry: Try a clearer photo or scan
  • Text rotated: Use the rotate button to correct orientation
  • Low contrast: The system works best with dark text on light backgrounds
  • Browser issue: Try refreshing the page or using Chrome/Edge

Document Not Loading?

  • File too large: Maximum file size is 50MB
  • Wrong format: Ensure file is JPG, PNG, or PDF
  • Corrupted file: Try re-scanning or re-downloading

Timeline Events Not Appearing?

  • Check Timeline Builder: Events may already be there
  • Refresh the page: Sometimes a refresh is needed
  • Clear browser cache: Old cached data can cause issues

Still Having Issues?

If you continue experiencing problems, try using the AI Assistant (Copilot) for help, or contact support through the Help menu.
