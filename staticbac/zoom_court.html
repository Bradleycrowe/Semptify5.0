Virtual Court Etiquette
Important Rules
  • Address the judge as "Your Honor"
  • Speak clearly and pause for responses
  • Remain muted unless speaking
  • No eating, drinking, or smoking on camera
  • Minimize background distractions
  • Position camera at eye level

Connection Requirements

Minimum: 1.5 Mbps up/down
Recommended: 3+ Mbps for stable video
