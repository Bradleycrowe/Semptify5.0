📋 What is HOME Line?

HOME Line is Minnesota's only statewide tenant hotline. They provide free advice and counseling to tenants about their legal rights, help with landlord negotiations, and guidance on next steps.

✅ Why Call HOME Line First?

Before filing formal complaints, HOME Line counselors can help you understand your rights, determine if you have a valid complaint, and advise on the best strategy for your specific situation.

📞 How to Contact HOME Line

  1. Call the hotline: (612) 728-5767 or toll-free 1-866-866-3546
  2. Be ready with your info: Have your lease, any notices from landlord, and timeline of events ready
  3. Describe your situation: Tell them what happened, when it happened, and what you've tried so far
  4. Take notes: Write down the counselor's advice and any recommended next steps
  5. Ask for resources: Request any forms, sample letters, or referrals they recommend

✅ What HOME Line Can Help With

  • Understanding your lease and tenant rights
  • Security deposit disputes
  • Repair and habitability issues
  • Eviction defense and prevention
  • Rent increases and lease termination
  • Landlord harassment or retaliation
  • Utility shutoffs
  • Fair housing and discrimination concerns
  • Referrals to legal aid and other resources

📝 Before You Call - Gather This Info

  • Your lease agreement (if you have one)
  • Any written notices from your landlord
  • Photos or documentation of issues
  • Timeline of when problems started
  • Records of communication with your landlord
  • Names and contact info of witnesses

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is HOME Line really free?
Yes! HOME Line is a nonprofit organization. Their hotline services are completely free for Minnesota tenants.
Do I need to be a legal resident to call?
No. HOME Line helps all Minnesota renters regardless of immigration status.
Can they represent me in court?
HOME Line provides advice and counseling but cannot represent you in court. They can refer you to Legal Aid or other legal services if needed.
What if I can't call during their hours?
You can email info@homelinemn.org or use their online tenant hotline form at homelinemn.org.
