BBB Complaint Tips

  • Be factual and specific - stick to dates, amounts, and events
  • Keep emotions out of the complaint text
  • Include all contact attempts with the business
  • Be clear about what resolution you want
  • Attach supporting documents if possible
  • Respond promptly if BBB or business contacts you
  • Consider also leaving a review on Google, Yelp, etc.
